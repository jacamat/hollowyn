The New Game for iPad

Hollowyn is a beautifully illustrated, immersive, open-world roleplay game where you can create unique characters, complete quests, collect magical items, and challenge your friends in missions and puzzles. Hollowyn is designed for ages 10+, and will be available on iOS devices beginning in winter, 2014.
